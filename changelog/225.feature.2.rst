`~stixpy.product.sources.flarelist.FlareList.plot_locations` gained a new ``observer="carrington"`` mode (alias ``"hgc"``) that plots flare positions in Heliographic Carrington coordinates. Because the Carrington prime meridian rotates with the Sun, a persistent active region keeps the same longitude across many days, making catalogues that span weeks or months readable as a single rotation-fixed scatter rather than a streak smeared by solar rotation.
